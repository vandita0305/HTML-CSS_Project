Ganga Aarti is an important ritual held on a grand scale every morning and evening on the banks of the holy Ganges. Ganga Aarti helps one attain Moksha and liberation of the soul.

One of the oldest ghats in Varanasi. It is believed that if a person is cremated here, he immediately attains moksha. Manikarnika is one of the five major land oldest pilgrimages and ghats of Kashi.
